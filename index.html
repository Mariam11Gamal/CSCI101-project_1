Cologne Cathedral Cologne Cathedral, officially the Cathedral Church of Saint Peter, is a prominent landmark in Cologne, Germany. It's one of the largest Gothic churches in northern Europe and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Brandenburg Gate he Brandenburg Gate is an iconic landmark in Berlin, Germany. It's a neoclassical monument that was once a city gate and has become a symbol of German history and unity.

Neuschwanstein Castle Neuschwanstein Castle is a 19th-century fairy tale castle perched on a rugged hill
in the Bavarian Alps of Germany. It was built by King Ludwig II of Bavaria as a personal retreat
and a monument to medieval chivalry and romance.
