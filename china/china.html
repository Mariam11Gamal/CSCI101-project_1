The Great Wall of China

The Great Wall of China is an ancient military defense project that is prominent and rare in human architectural history. It is famous in the world for its ancient history, the magnitude of its fortifications, its greatness and its power. The construction of the Great Wall began during the Spring, Autumn and Warring States era more than 2,000 years ago.

The Forbidden City

It was once a "palace" city where ordinary people were forbidden entry. The Forbidden City was the imperial palace of the Ming and Qing Dynasties for 560 years till 1911. 24 emperors lived there. Listed as World Cultural Heritage, it also houses many Chinese cultural and historical relics and treasures.

West Lake in Hangzhou

Hangzhou is renowned as China's "paradise on earth" owing to its beautifully crafted landscape West Lake was created after the Chinese love for garden-style parks for recreation. To admire the beauty of West Lake, you can leisurely bike around the lake.

China's Giant Pandas

The giant panda, native to China, is a beloved symbol of wildlife conservation. Known for its distinct black and white fur, it primarily inhabits the mountainous regions of Sichuan, Shaanxi, and Gansu. Pandas primarily feed on bamboo, which constitutes about 99% of their diet. Conservation efforts have helped increase their population
